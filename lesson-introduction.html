Greek Alphabet & Reading Readiness

The Greek letters are στοιχεῖα, the elements or building blocks of reading. This unit is an initiation into Greek signs, sounds, marks, typing, and early decoding, so Lesson 1 can begin with confidence rather than guesswork.

Learn Ancient Greek with Xenophon treats language as a way into character, action, custom, and judgment. Unit 0 begins with the smallest readable signs and moves toward simple Greek words that already belong to that larger world.

By the end of this unit, you will be able to...

  • recognize all Greek letters
  • match uppercase and lowercase forms
  • hear and identify letter sounds
  • recognize diphthongs and major consonant combinations
  • understand breathings, accents, and iota subscript
  • type polytonic Greek
  • read simple Greek words aloud
  • pass the Reading Readiness Check
